Random Forest Meta-Learner Stacking Spatiotemporal Analysis Python Meteosat Imagery
Task Aufgabe

Engineered a pipeline integrating Ground Sensors, Weather Radars, and Meteosat satellite imagery to bridge accuracy gaps in traditional rain gauges across heterogeneous data sources.
