## 🔬 Projects & Research

### Project 01 · Rainfall Estimation via Heterogeneous Data Fusion & ML
> ★ **Peer-Reviewed · Published · IRJMETS · March 2025**

**The problem:** Traditional rain gauges are inaccurate because they rely on a single data source.

**What I built:** A pipeline integrating Ground Sensors, Weather Radars, and Meteosat satellite imagery — three sources that had never been combined this way. I developed a Hierarchical Probabilistic Ensemble Classifier (HPEC) using Random Forest and meta-learner stacking to combine multiple weak signals into one highly accurate prediction.

**The result: 0.58 POD — outperformed the industry-standard KED model.** Directly scalable to flood early-warning systems and landslide risk alerts.

`Random Forest` `Meta-Learner Stacking` `Python` `Spatiotemporal Analysis` `Meteosat Imagery`
